My Hobbies

Below is a list of all of my favorite hobbies. Do we have something in common? Let me know!


► READING

Reading is definitely one of my most favorite past times. I wasn't into reading for a very long time. The last time I got into a really good book that I absolutely couldn't put down, I was in highschool reading the Twilight series with my best friend at the time. Sadly, I wasn't able to finish the series due to my parents religious beliefs and being forbidden from reading the rest. Today, as an adult, I own the box set and several special editions of the Twilight series! No shame!


► Video Games

Video games is another favorite hobby of mine. My primary gaming platforms are PC, Steam Deck, and Nintendo Switch. I love sandbox games the most where I am able to build things, explore, and befriend creatures. Ark Survival Evolved, Pal World, Pokemon, Stardew Valley, Ooblets, and Animal Crossing are a few of my absolute favorite games of all time!


► Web Development

Web development is actually one of my favorite hobbies! I have my own website which is a more personal project, and due to my anonymity on it I'd prefer not to share it here. But I can say that it is made with Wordpress, and it is a small web project I feel really passionate about. The small web has become a sort of niche where people make their own personal creative website just for fun! They are often have elements of nostalgia from Web 1.0 (think early Myspace era, Geocities, etc) when the web wasn't so commercialized and people used the web more for fun rather than business. This era was a time when the internet had a lot more soul and human spirit. So the small web, in a nutshell, is a movement of people who want to bring that back, putting people and creativity over profits. This is one of my favorite geeky past times!


► Beach Combing

During the summer when the weather is warmer, I love going to the beach and looking for sea treasures, such as sea glass, shells, hag stones, and pretty rocks. You never know what kind of treasures you might find!


► DIY

Last but not least, I absolutely LOVE DIY projects and crafting. I make jewelry, crochet, occasionally sew, and just love creative projects. I think this is why web development is perfect for me as a professional career, because I'm a builder, and I love being creative; whether it be making crafts with my hands or graphic design, or building entire websites. I thrive in projects where I get to be creative and use my imagination.
